## The Opportunity

The global music royalty market represents a **$28.5 billion annual opportunity**, yet artists struggle to track, collect, and optimize their earnings across hundreds of streaming platforms. **SUPER GOAT ROYALTY APP** solves this problem with a comprehensive AI-powered platform.

---

## The Problem

### Current Industry Pain Points

1. **Fragmented Royalty Data** - Artists must check dozens of platforms manually
2. **Lost Revenue** - Estimated $2.5B in unclaimed royalties annually
3. **No Real-Time Visibility** - 90+ day delays in royalty reporting
4. **Complex Rights Management** - Multiple PROs, publishers, and collection societies
5. **No AI-Powered Insights** - No tools to predict and optimize earnings

### Who Suffers Most

- **Independent Artists** - 40% of the market, least served
- **Producers & Songwriters** - Complex split sheets, delayed payments
- **Small Publishers** - No access to enterprise tracking tools
- **Emerging Markets** - Global distribution without global collection
